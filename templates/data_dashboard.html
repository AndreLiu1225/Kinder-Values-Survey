Dynamic values results

Openness to Change

Relations among these 10 broad personal values are dynamic. Actions pursuing one value "have" consequences that conflict with some values but are congrugent with others. For openness to change, it is determined by the basic values self-direction, stimulation and hedonism. If you have answered highly for these 3 values, this shows your emphasis on independence of thought, action and feelings and readiness for change.

Conservation

Conservation values are constrast to values of the former. Conservation is determined by the basic values conformity, tradition and security. This dimension emphasis order, self-restriction for stability and preservation of the past, and resistance to change.

Self-Transcendence

Self-Transcendence values consist of universalism and benevolence, showing a strong emphasis on concern for the welfare and interests of others. Which is a polar opposite of the next dimension below, self-enhancement.

Self-Enhancement

Self-Enhancement values are in direct conflict with self-transendence values. With a stronger emphasis on the pursuit of one's own interests and relative success and dominance over others. It is defined by the basic values of power and achievement.
